ABOUT

GAI presents:

The first "Global Appathon"! This is a 12-hour fun sized hackathon for mobile development, open to all students to work on mobile projects.

We Offer:

Come for FREE food, FREE swag, and FREE fun! There will be category prizes for projects that are developed entirely during the 12-hour period, but anyone working on a mobile project is welcome to come and benefit from the assistance of our instructors!

Engineering Product Innovation Center 750 Comm. Ave
